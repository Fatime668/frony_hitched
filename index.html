About

Summer 2023 - fully booked!

This stunning Grade ll listed Georgian mansion amidst landscaped gardens offers a historic and exclusive setting for your wedding. Close to Richmond Park, Grove House has several beautiful rooms in which your celebrations can take place. Working with our list of accredited suppliers and dedicated on site events team we can accommodate a variety of weddings. The team, with their years of experience are on hand to help plan your perfect day and ensure you and your guests will leave with memories that everyone will be talking about for years to come.

Facilities and Capacity

Grove House can facilitate wedding ceremonies of up to 150 guests, increasing to 200 for evening celebrations. On arrival, guests are welcomed into the Portrait Room, where the main wedding ceremony can take place. Couples can coordinate their dates and times with Wandsworth, the local registrars. Groups arriving from a church service are greeted with drinks and canapes on the grounds. Music and entertainment options can be added here to heighten the ambience.
After spending time mingling and socializing, guests are shown into the Adam Room, where the wedding cake is on display. From here, everyone is guided to the Portrait Room to enjoy a three-course meal from one of the venue's special suppliers. Once the wedding speeches finish, the Terrace Room is transformed into a dancing area and full-service bar for the evening entertainment.

Services Offered

The staff at Grove House prides themselves on offering a high calibre of service. Couples can work with a designated event manager to organize the wedding according to their preferences. On the big day, this supervisor will oversee the celebration from start to finish. The venue partners with several catering teams in the vicinity, allowing to-be-weds to select a suitable menu. As the day draws to a close, guests are asked to vacate the manor by midnight. From here, they can book an overnight stay at the conference centre on the premises, which is a short walk away. The mansion is available to hire on Saturdays at any time of the year.
